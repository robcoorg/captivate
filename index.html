Core Platform

Everything Your Practice Needs, Bundled Together

One platform. One price. Every tool your med spa needs to operate, grow, and stay compliant — included from day one, with no add-ons required.

HIPAA-Compliant EMR

Integrated photo management with before/after galleries, injection-site markup, and digital SOAP notes — all synced to the client profile and accessible at point of care.

Smart Resource Scheduling

Book providers, rooms, and devices simultaneously — preventing double-booking and idle capacity. Automated reminders keep your schedule full and your patients informed.

Omni-Channel Inbox

2-way SMS, VoIP calls, and web chat unified in one inbox — so your team never misses a patient message, regardless of how they reach out.

AI Patient Re-Engagement

An intelligent agent that identifies patients who haven't returned and reaches out with treatment-specific suggestions based on their history — not a generic mass email.

Medical-First Compliance

Good Faith Exam tracking and provider supervision logs built in as core settings — keeping your practice audit-ready without adding complexity or headcount.

Integrated POS & Memberships

PCI-compliant payments with membership support, package tracking, and a self-service client portal — so patients can manage their own accounts without calling the front desk.
