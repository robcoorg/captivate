Platform Features

Clinical-Grade Tools. One Flat Rate.

Everything your med spa needs to operate, grow, and stay compliant — bundled together, not sold separately.

Medical Records

An EMR Built for the Way Aesthetic Practices Work

Captivate was designed medical-first, with documentation tools that match how aesthetic providers actually deliver care — not adapted from a general-purpose system.

Before/After Photo Management

Integrated gallery with injection-site markup and annotation tools — all linked to the client record.

Digital SOAP Notes

Structured templates that sync directly to the client profile at point of care.

Automated Intake Forms

HIPAA-secure forms completed by patients before they arrive — no clipboards, no scanning.

GFE & Supervision Logs

Good Faith Exam tracking and provider oversight logs — built in as a core setting, not a workaround.

Smart Scheduling

Book the Room, the Provider, and the Device — All at Once

Standard scheduling tools only book providers. Captivate books providers, rooms, and devices simultaneously — preventing the double-booking conflicts that cost practices thousands each month in lost revenue and rescheduling friction.

Room & Device Linking

Every booking is tied to a specific room and device — no conflicts, no idle equipment.

Automated Reminders

SMS and email reminders that reduce no-shows by up to 40%.

Online Self-Booking

Clients book 24/7 from any device with real-time availability across all resources.

Omni-Channel Inbox

Every Patient Conversation in One Place

2-way SMS, VoIP calls, and web chat — unified in a single inbox so your team never loses track of a patient message, regardless of how they reach out. Included in your plan, not sold separately.

2-Way SMS

Reply directly from the patient's profile. Up to 1,000 texts/month included in mid-tier plans.

VoIP Calls

Make and receive calls within the platform — call logs sync automatically to the client record.

Web Chat Widget

Capture leads from your website and route them directly to your team's inbox.

AI Patient Re-Engagement

Outreach That Feels Personal — Because It Is

Generic re-engagement campaigns get ignored. Captivate's AI agent scans your patient database, identifies clients who haven't returned in 4+ months, and crafts outreach that references their specific treatment history — dramatically improving response and rebooking rates.

History-Based Suggestions

AI recommends the next logical treatment based on what each patient has received before.

Automated Lifecycle Sequences

Timed outreach that recovers thousands in missed repeat visits every month.

Campaign Analytics

Track open rates, booking conversions, and revenue recovered per campaign.

Payments & Memberships

Revenue Tools That Work as Hard as You Do

From PCI-compliant card processing to flexible membership programs and self-service client portals — Captivate handles the revenue side of your practice with the same care as the clinical side.

PCI-Compliant Payments

Accept cards, contactless payments, and financing options — all within a secure, compliant payment environment built for healthcare.

Flexible Memberships

Rollover credits, package tracking, and automated renewal billing — including "Bank Your Botox" style programs that keep clients committed and returning.

Self-Service Client Portal

Clients upgrade, pause, or swap membership benefits from their phone — reducing front-desk calls and giving patients the autonomy they expect.

Package & Gift Card Tracking
