Patient Experience

The Touchpoints That Build Lasting Loyalty

The best med spas don't chase patients — they create experiences so seamless that clients return on their own. Captivate automates the touchpoints that build that loyalty, from first booking to long-term retention.

Digital Intake Forms

HIPAA-secure intake forms completed by patients before they arrive. Everything syncs to the client profile automatically — no clipboards, no scanning, no manual entry.

Membership Self-Service

Clients upgrade, pause, or swap membership benefits from their phone — no front-desk calls, no friction. Rollover credits and automated renewal billing keep retention effortless.

AI Re-Engagement

An intelligent agent that identifies patients who haven't returned and sends outreach that references their specific treatment history — timely, relevant, and personal.

Smart Reminders

Automated appointment reminders via SMS and email that reduce no-shows by up to 40% — customizable timing and messaging per treatment type.
