Our Story

Built by Practitioners, for Practitioners

Captivate was born from a simple frustration: the tools available to med spa owners were either too expensive, too complex, or built for a different kind of business entirely.

Our Mission

Clinical Serenity Shouldn't Stop at the Treatment Room Door

The best aesthetic practices create an experience of calm, trust, and precision for their patients. We believe the operations behind that experience should feel the same way — seamless, intelligent, and quietly powerful.

Captivate was built to remove the operational noise that keeps practice owners up at night: the scheduling conflicts, the compliance paperwork, the patients who slip through the cracks, the software bills that keep climbing. We replace all of that with one platform that simply works.

"The best technology disappears into the background and lets the human experience come forward."
What We Stand For

The Principles Behind Every Decision We Make

These aren't marketing words. They're the filters we use when deciding what to build, how to price it, and how to support the practices that trust us.

01

Medical-First, Always

Every feature we build starts with the clinical reality of running a med spa — not a day spa, not a salon. Compliance, documentation, and oversight are core, not afterthoughts.

02

Transparent Pricing

The price you see is the price you pay. We don't believe in base rates that balloon with add-ons. One flat rate, everything included, no surprises on your bill.

03

Patient-First Design

Every workflow we design is evaluated from the patient's perspective first. If it creates friction for the people your practice serves, we don't ship it.

04

Radical Simplicity

Powerful software doesn't have to be complicated. We obsess over making complex operations feel effortless — for owners, staff, and patients alike.

05

Partnership Over Transaction

When you join Captivate, you get a migration team, an onboarding specialist, and ongoing support — not a knowledge base and a ticket queue. We succeed when you do.

06

Built to Last

We're building a long-term platform for a growing industry. That means making decisions that are right for our customers over years — not quarters.

Why We Exist

The Med Spa Industry Deserves Better Software

The aesthetic medicine industry is growing rapidly — and the operational demands on practice owners are growing with it. More patients, more compliance requirements, more staff to coordinate, more revenue to protect.

Yet the software available to most practices hasn't kept pace. Tools are fragmented, pricing is opaque, and the features that matter most — HIPAA compliance, GFE tracking, AI-driven retention — are either missing or locked behind expensive add-ons.

Captivate exists to change that. We built the platform we wished existed: medically rigorous, operationally complete, and priced so that any practice — from a solo injector to a multi-location group — can access the same quality of tools.
