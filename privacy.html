Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Captivate ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and their patients. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Captivate platform and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account registration information (name, email address, practice name)
  • Billing and payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Patient data entered into the platform by your practice (governed by our BAA)
  • Communications you send to us via contact forms or email

2. HIPAA Compliance

Captivate is a HIPAA-covered platform. All Protected Health Information (PHI) entered into the platform is handled in accordance with HIPAA requirements. We execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with every customer practice prior to handling PHI. Patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit and is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Captivate platform
  • Process payments and send billing communications
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send product updates and service announcements (you may opt out at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, role-based access controls, and regular third-party security audits. Our infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2 Type II certified cloud providers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Upon cancellation, you may request a full export of your data within 30 days. Patient records are retained in accordance with applicable state medical record retention laws.

6. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate the platform, including Stripe (payments), Twilio (SMS/VoIP), and AWS (infrastructure). Each of these providers is bound by data processing agreements that restrict their use of your data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@captivate.icu.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
privacy@captivate.icu
